# Slide 5 Prompt

Title: `Layer 1 Questions Are Not Layer 2 Criteria`

Show a split prior-auth logic slide. Left panel title: `Layer 1: questions`. Show a generic questionnaire stack with chips `pain duration?`, `therapy tried?`, `red flags?`, `imaging?`. Label it `answers without the rule`.

Right panel title: `Layer 2: criteria logic`. Show a policy logic map with chips `thresholds`, `AND / OR`, `timeframes`, `exceptions`, `why it matters`. Include a small decision path ending in `evidence gap` or `criteria met`.

Bottom callout exact text: `A pile of questions does not tell the agent how answers are used.`

Do not imply payers must expose proprietary source code. The point is clinical criteria transparency: enough logic to assemble truthful evidence and explain gaps.
