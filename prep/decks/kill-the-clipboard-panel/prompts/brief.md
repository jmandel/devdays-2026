## Shared Style

White background. Broad heavy non-condensed sans-serif titles, top-left, never horizontally squeezed. Strong contrast charcoal text. Use rounded cards, workflow arrows, medical-record artifacts, and clear icons. Keep slides legible from the back of a room: short labels, few words, no dense paragraphs.
